About Stark Industries

Meet some of the Founding Members of Stark Industries

Tony Stark

Meet Tony Stark, the visionary CEO of Stark Industries. Renowned for his brilliance and engineering prowess, Stark has led the company to unparalleled success. His innovations, including the iconic Iron Man suit, have redefined the limits of technology.

Pepper Potts

Pepper Potts, not just the wife of Tony Stark but a significant board member at Stark Industries. Her strategic acumen and dedication to sustainability have played a pivotal role in the company's growth. Potts is a driving force behind Stark Industries' commitment to a greener and more responsible future.

Happy Hogan

Happy Hogan, another significant board member at Stark Industries. With a background in security and unwavering loyalty to Tony Stark, Hogan ensures the safety and success of the company. His contributions extend beyond the boardroom, making him an integral part of the Stark Industries family.
